# CRM Opportunity Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
CRM Opportunity Analysis is a data analytics project focused on evaluating sales opportunities, revenue performance, and CRM data. The project analyzes opportunity pipelines, win rates, conversion metrics, and industry trends to deliver actionable business insights through interactive dashboards and KPI tracking.

## Objectives
* Monitor sales opportunity performance
* Analyze revenue trends and business growth
* Track win rates and conversion metrics
* Identify industry-wise opportunity distribution
* Support data-driven decision-making

## Tools Used
* Power BI
* Microsoft Excel

## Key KPIs
* Expected Revenue
* Win Rate
* Active Opportunities
* Total Opportunities
* Conversion Rate

## Dashboard Features
* Revenue trend analysis by year
* Opportunity analysis by industry
* Active vs Total opportunities comparison
* Won vs Lost opportunities tracking
* Top-performing opportunities visualization
* Interactive filters for Date, Industry, and Stage

## Business Insights
* Identified industries contributing the highest number of opportunities.
* Analyzed revenue growth patterns across multiple years.
* Evaluated opportunity conversion and win performance.
* Tracked sales pipeline effectiveness through KPI monitoring.
